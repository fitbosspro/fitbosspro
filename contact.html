Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to emails?
We aim to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, phone calls or social media DMs may get a faster response.
Do you offer personal training or coaching?
Currently, we focus on providing evidence-based information through our articles. We don't offer one-on-coaching, but we can recommend qualified professionals in your area.
Can I contribute an article to FitBoss Pro?
We welcome guest contributions from qualified professionals. Please email us with your credentials and article ideas for review.
Do you accept advertising or sponsorships?
Yes, we carefully vet partners who align with our evidence-based mission. Contact us for our media kit and advertising guidelines.
Where do you get your research?
We review peer-reviewed studies from journals like Journal of Strength and Conditioning Research, Sports Medicine, Medicine & Science in Sports & Exercise, and others.
Can I suggest a topic for an article?
Absolutely! We love reader suggestions. Use the contact form to let us know what you'd like to learn about.
