‚ùì Used EV FAQ

Do used EVs qualify for tax credits?
Yes‚Äîthe Inflation Reduction Act offers up to $4,000 or 30% of the price (whichever is lower) for used EVs under $25,000 that are at least 2 years old. Income limits apply .
Is certified pre-owned worth it for an EV?
Absolutely. CPO adds manufacturer-backed warranty, thorough inspection, and often roadside assistance. For EVs, battery health verification is a huge plus .
How much battery degradation is normal?
Most EVs lose 5-10% of capacity after 100,000 miles. A well-maintained EV with 50,000 miles should have 95%+ battery health .
Should I worry about previous battery recalls?
Check that recalls were performed. The Chevy Bolt recall resulted in brand-new batteries for many owners‚Äîthat's actually a plus .
