Two Luxury EVs, Two Different Philosophies

The Lexus RZ, thoroughly updated for 2026, offers more range, a new F Sport performance variant, and Lexus's legendary refinement at a much lower price. Here's how they compare.

The Lexus RZ starts at $47,295, a whopping $28,000 less than the base BMW iX xDrive45 . That's a massive difference . Even the top-tier RZ 550e F Sport at $58,295 undercuts the base iX . The BMW iX, while expensive, offers a more premium interior, greater range, and more power‚Äîeven at entry level .

‚ö° Range & Efficiency: BMW Leads

The BMW iX xDrive60 achieves up to 364 miles of range, while the iX xDrive45 offers 312 miles‚Äîboth besting the Lexus . The Lexus RZ 350e now offers 302 miles (base FWD), a huge improvement, but the dual-motor 450e drops to 261 miles, and the performance-oriented 550e F Sport manages just 225 miles . For long-distance driving, the BMW is the clear winner.

üîå Charging: Lexus Gains Supercharger Access

For 2026, the Lexus RZ comes standard with a NACS port, granting direct access to Tesla's Supercharger network‚Äîa massive advantage for road-trippers . The BMW iX retains its CCS port and charges at up to 195 kW (vs. the RZ's 150 kW), meaning faster peak charging speeds where CCS networks are available . Both achieve 10-80% in about 30 minutes.

ü™ë Interior & Luxury: BMW's Tech vs. Lexus's Simplicity

The BMW iX features a minimalist yet opulent cabin with a curved 14.9-inch display, available Bowers & Wilkins sound system, and exceptionally quiet highway cruising‚ÄîRAC measured just 65 decibels . The Lexus RZ focuses on simplicity with an 11.4-inch touchscreen and synthetic leather upholstery. It's refined but less lavish . The iX also offers more cargo space (77.9 cu ft max vs. about 18.4 cu ft in the RZ).

üöó Performance: BMW Dominates

The BMW iX M70 produces 650 hp and hits 60 mph in 3.6 seconds . Even the base iX xDrive45 delivers 402 hp and 4.9-second 0-60 . The Lexus RZ 550e F Sport offers 402 hp and a 4.1-second 0-60, matching the base BMW's power . The RZ 450e is much slower at 4.9 seconds with only 308 hp . For driving enthusiasts, the BMW is the clear choice.

Our Take

The BMW iX is the superior luxury EV in nearly every objective measure‚Äîperformance, range, interior quality. But it costs a fortune. The Lexus RZ, especially with its 2026 updates, offers incredible value and now includes Supercharger access. If you can afford it, the iX is the ultimate expression of a luxury electric SUV. If you're budget-conscious, the RZ delivers 90% of the refinement at half the price.
