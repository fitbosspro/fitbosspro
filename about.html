About AutoTimegist

Your trusted source for professional car reviews, automotive news, expert buying guides, and maintenance tips since 2015.

Our Story

AutoTimegist was founded in 2026 by a group of automotive journalists and engineers who shared a common frustration: too much car content was either superficial marketing fluff or overly technical jargon that left everyday drivers confused. We envisioned a platform that combined rigorous, professional testing with clear, helpful writing that actually answered the questions real car buyers have.

What started as a small blog run from a garage in Detroit has grown into one of the most trusted independent automotive resources online. We've tested over 800 vehicles, from affordable city cars to exotic supercars, and our reviews are read by millions of car shoppers every year. We've never accepted payment for positive reviews, and we never will. Our only allegiance is to our readers.

In 2026, we continue to expand our coverage to meet the rapidly changing automotive landscape. From the latest electric vehicles and hybrids to traditional gasoline-powered cars, we're here to help you make informed decisions.
